RepLog — Gym Tracker

The no-nonsense way to track your training.

RepLog is a fast, simple workout logger. Follow built-in routines (Push/Pull/Legs, Upper/Lower, Full Body, StrongLifts 5×5, Bro Split, A/B Day, Booty Builder) or build your own, log your sets in seconds, and watch your weights climb. Everything is stored on your device — no account, no ads, no subscription.

FAQ

Where is my data stored?
Only on your device. RepLog has no servers and no account.

Is it free?
Yes — RepLog is free, with no ads or subscription.

Can I add my own exercises and routines?
Yes. Tap “Add custom exercise,” or build a full custom routine in the Routines tab.
