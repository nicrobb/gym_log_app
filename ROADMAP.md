# Gym Log — Roadmap

## Monetization strategy

**Launch free. Keep a Pro unlock in the back pocket.**

A brand-new app from an unknown developer with no ratings won't sell upfront —
the category is full of free, polished competitors (Shrug, Strong, Hevy). A
price wall kills discovery, installs, reviews, and word-of-mouth. Free also
skips Apple's Paid Apps Agreement + banking/tax setup.

- **v1 (now):** Free, no in-app purchases, no ads. Maximize installs + reviews.
- **Later, if monetizing:** optional one-time **"Pro" unlock** (in-app purchase)
  — users who already love the free app convert far better than strangers
  paying upfront, and it earns more than a $4.99 wall ever would.

### Candidate Pro features
- iCloud sync / backup across devices
- Progress charts (per-exercise 1RM trend, volume over time)
- Unlimited custom routines (free tier could cap them)
- Plate calculator
- Rest timer with notifications
- Export workout history (CSV)

## Feature ideas (post-launch)

- Exercise history view — tap an exercise to see all past sets/progression
- Apple Health integration (write workouts)
- Supersets / circuit grouping in routines
- Bodyweight / measurements tracking
- Warmup-set flag (distinct from working sets)
- Per-set RPE / notes
- Dumbbell-only and home/bodyweight built-in routines
