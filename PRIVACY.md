# Privacy Policy — Gym Log

_Last updated: 12 June 2026_

Gym Log is designed to be private by default.

## What we collect

**Nothing.** Gym Log does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

- All your workouts, routines, exercises, and settings are stored **only on your
  device**, using local storage.
- We do not have servers, accounts, logins, or analytics.
- We do not use advertising or tracking SDKs.
- Your data never leaves your phone unless you back it up yourself through
  Apple's own iCloud device backup.

## Data you create

Workout logs, custom exercises, and routines stay on your device. Deleting the
app removes this data permanently. We cannot recover it because we never have it.

## Children's privacy

Gym Log does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL and
the "Last updated" date above will change.
